About

Hey guys, my name is Derin Joseph and I am a problem solver. I started programming when I was 16 and I ended up making a career out of it. I am currently modernizing State Farm's auto insurance applications and working on a stealth start-up with some friends. I am always interested in learning new technologies and helping other people enter the field and/or progress their careers.

My passion lies in creating products that not only solve complex problems but also make a meaningful difference in people's lives. I am driven by the desire to empower teams to innovate, collaborate, and build solutions that have a lasting impact. By combining strategic vision with hands-on expertise, I aim to lead initiatives that inspire change, foster growth, and enhance the everyday experiences of users around the world.
